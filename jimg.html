Jim Gibson 


Jim provides the hard punch to the guitar sound on our heavier
numbers, while giving us a warm acoustic tone on the softer songs. Formerly a drummer, Jim switched to playing guitar after being injured in a fall and requiring a wheelchair for mobility. He plays Jackson, Dean, and Schecter guitars through a Peavy 4 x12 cabinet powered by a Marshall Valvestate head.
