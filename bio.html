Fools Errand

Who we are:


Fools Errand was formed in 2014 by founding members bassist Don Foscoe and guitarist Jim Gibson. Guitarist Chris Rankin joined the project soon after, adding a filling tone to the bands sound. Not long after, vocalist Dave Milliern was added to the band bringing his vocal prowess and great stage presence to front the act. After being through a few drummers, Harold Blum secured the position with impeccable timing
and his thunderous beats. Vocalist Theosevia Demertzis added her vocals to the line up, joining us in mid 2017, giving our sound a powerful vocal addition, as well as providing the band with a wider array of options when it comes to song selection. Dave Villotti stepped in as our new vocalist when Big D (Dave Milliern) had to step down due to health issues. When Don moved forward in his career and took a
hiatus from the stage we recruited Jim Seaman to fill the open position of bass guitar.


Fools Errand performs covers of your favorite rock/hard rock/heavy metal songs from the previous 5 decades - from The Doors to Godsmack as well as some songs we have put our own spin on. With the addition of Theosevia to the line-up, we have expanded our song list to include many female fronted songs.
